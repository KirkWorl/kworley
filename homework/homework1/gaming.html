Machine Learning in Gaming - A New AI

Gaming is an ever changing industry with constantly updating artificial intelligence that can utilize machine learning to create greater challenges.
The earliest instance of artificial intelligence dates back to 1952, where a rules based AI was created to play perfect tic-tac-toe games. Since then, artificial intelligence has been a major staple in the gaming industry, allowing for new and innovative depth in gaming experiences. This surge in the industry in turn led to a highly adaptive machine learning need for developers working with this kind of technology. One example of this kind of machine learning being utilized to conquer the gaming world is a neural network built to learn the game 'Super Mario World.'
